Biography

Hi! I'm originally from the San Francisco Bay Area. I worked for 20 years as a journalist for many publications including The Wall Street Journal, where I was a full-time staff reporter for seven years and a roving foreign correspondent for two. That was a fun job: I reported on the state of technology while riding my bicycle around the world, including over the Himalayas to Everest Base Camp.

I now run a company dedicated to science education, with a focus on getting more girls involved in STEM. Looking forward to using my new skills in my own company and in my next adventure!

In 2010 I founded The Great Adventure Lab, a STEM enrichment company for kids grades K-5 in the Washington, D.C. area.We've taught more than 15,000 kids how to program since then!

We offer classes in more than a dozen subjects including Lego Robotics, Video Game Programming, Engineering, Electronics, Chemistry, Storybook Science, Nature Science and the Art of Math.
